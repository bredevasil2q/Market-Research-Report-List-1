<p><p>The Human Embryonic Stem Cell Assay market encompasses various types focused on evaluating the properties and functions of stem cells. Viability/Cytotoxicity assays assess cell health and survival rates. Isolation & Purification techniques are used to obtain specific stem cell populations. Cell Identification ensures the accuracy of stem cells' origin. Proliferation assays measure growth rates, while Differentiation assays evaluate the ability to transform into specialized cell types. Function assays assess the biological roles of stem cells, and Apoptosis assays examine programmed cell death processes.</p></p>
