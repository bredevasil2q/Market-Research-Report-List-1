<p><p>The cytogenetic instrument market encompasses various techniques for analyzing chromosomes and genetic material. Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) visualizes specific DNA sequences using fluorescent probes, aiding in genetic diagnostics. Comparative genomic hybridization (CGH) compares the genomic content of test samples to reference samples to identify genetic imbalances. Flow cytometry allows for rapid cell analysis and Sorting based on their physical and chemical properties. Multiplex-FISH enhances detection by enabling simultaneous visualization of multiple targets, improving diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
