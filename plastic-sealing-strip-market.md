<p><p>The Plastic Sealing Strip market is segmented into two main types: Continuous Vulcanization and Discontinuous Vulcanization. Continuous Vulcanization Plastic Sealing Strips involve a manufacturing process where rubber is cured continuously, resulting in a uniform and consistent product suitable for high-volume applications. In contrast, Discontinuous Vulcanization Plastic Sealing Strips are produced in batches, allowing for flexibility in design and smaller production runs, making them ideal for specialized or custom applications. Each type caters to different industry needs and production scales.</p></p>
