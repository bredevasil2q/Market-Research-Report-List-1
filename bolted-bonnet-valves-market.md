<p><ul><li>Bolted Bonnet Check Valves</li><li>Bolted Bonnet Globe Valves</li><li>Bolted Bonnet Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bolted bonnet valves market consists of three primary types: bolted bonnet check valves, bolted bonnet globe valves, and bolted bonnet gate valves. Bolted bonnet check valves prevent backflow, ensuring one-way fluid movement. Bolted bonnet globe valves regulate flow with precision, often used in throttling applications. Bolted bonnet gate valves provide a straight flow path, allowing for full flow when fully opened, typically employed in on/off service. Each type is vital for efficiency and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
