<p><ul><li>Physico-Mechanical Methods</li><li>Chemical Methods</li><li>Physico-Chemical Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fragrances microencapsulation market is categorized into three main types: physico-mechanical methods, chemical methods, and physico-chemical methods. Physico-mechanical methods involve the physical processes of coating fragrance materials with polymers or other matrices to control their release. Chemical methods utilize chemical reactions to form encapsulated structures that enhance stability and longevity. Physico-chemical methods combine both physical and chemical techniques, enabling more complex encapsulation systems that improve fragrance protection and diffusion, catering to diverse applications in cosmetics, textiles, and food industries.</p></p>
