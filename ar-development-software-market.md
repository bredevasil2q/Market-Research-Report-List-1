<p><p>The AR development software market is primarily divided into two types: AR SDK software and AR WYSIWYG editor software. In contrast, AR WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) editor software allows users to design AR experiences visually, facilitating easier creation without extensive coding expertise. Both types cater to different user needs, promoting AR innovation and accessibility.</p></p>
