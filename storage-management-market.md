<p><p>Storage management encompasses two primary market types: cloud and on-premises solutions. Cloud storage offers scalability and flexibility by allowing users to store and access data remotely over the internet, typically on a subscription basis. This model reduces the need for physical hardware and facilitates easy collaboration.
