<p><strong>The Air Suspension Electronic Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Controller</li><li>Passive Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Suspension Electronic Controller market is divided into two main types: active controllers and passive controllers. Active controllers dynamically adjust the air pressure in suspension systems based on real-time driving conditions, providing enhanced ride comfort and stability. In contrast, passive controllers maintain a fixed air pressure level, offering simpler and more cost-effective solutions. Both types cater to different vehicle performance needs, with active controllers being ideal for high-performance applications, while passive controllers serve more traditional, budget-conscious vehicles.</p></p>
