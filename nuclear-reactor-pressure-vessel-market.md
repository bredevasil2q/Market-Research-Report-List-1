<p><p>The Nuclear Reactor Pressure Vessel (RPV) market comprises various types of reactors, each serving distinct functions. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water to transfer heat, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) allow water to boil in the reactor core. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water for better efficiency. Gas-cooled Reactors (GCR) use gas as a coolant, Low-Temperature Gas-Cooled Reactors (LWGR) operate at lower temperatures, and Fast Breeder Reactors (FBR) generate more fuel than consumed, contributing to sustainable nuclear energy development.</p></p>
