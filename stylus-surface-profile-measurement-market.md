<p><p>Stylus surface profile measurement encompasses various market types, including inductive, piezoelectric, and photoelectric methods. Inductive measurement uses electromagnetic principles to gauge surface variations, offering high precision for conductive materials. Piezoelectric technology relies on the piezoelectric effect, converting mechanical strain into electrical signals for detailed surface profile analysis. Photoelectric methods utilize light to measure surface irregularities, beneficial for both conductive and non-conductive materials. Each type serves specific applications and industries, ensuring accurate and reliable surface analysis.</p></p>
