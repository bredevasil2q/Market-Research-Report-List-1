<p><strong>The Dissolved CO2 Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric</li><li>Thermal Conductivity</li><li>Infrared Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolved CO2 sensor market includes three main types: potentiometric, thermal conductivity, and infrared optics. Potentiometric sensors measure the change in voltage related to CO2 concentration, allowing for precise monitoring. Thermal conductivity sensors assess changes in heat transfer properties depending on CO2 levels, providing accurate readings in various conditions. Infrared optics sensors utilize the absorption of infrared light by CO2 molecules, enabling non-invasive and fast detection. Each type serves distinct applications in environmental monitoring, food packaging, and beverage industries.</p></p>
