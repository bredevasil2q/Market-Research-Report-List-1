<p><p>The flexible laminations adhesives market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Water-Based Adhesives** use water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easy clean-up, making them eco-friendly. </p><p>2. **Solvent-Based Adhesives** provide strong bonding and versatility but contain volatile compounds. </p><p>3. **Hot Melt Adhesives** apply as a solid and bond upon cooling, enabling quick assembly. </p><p>4. **Solventless Adhesives** eliminate solvents altogether, providing high efficiency and reduced environmental impact, ideal for high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific packaging and bonding needs.</p></p>
