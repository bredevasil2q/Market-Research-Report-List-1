<p><strong>The Deep Cycle Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VRLA</li><li>FLA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deep cycle battery market primarily features two types: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) and Flooded Lead Acid (FLA) batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and ideal for applications requiring consistent power supply, making them popular in renewable energy systems. In contrast, FLA batteries are vented and allow for the addition of water, offering more robust performance under high discharge rates, making them suitable for applications like forklifts and industrial machinery. Both types cater to diverse energy storage needs.</p></p>
