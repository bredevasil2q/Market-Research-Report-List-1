<p><p>The marker pen market can be categorized into oily (alcoholic) markers and water-based markers. Oily markers contain alcohol-based ink, providing vibrant colors and quick drying times, making them ideal for professional uses such as graphic design or crafting. They offer water and fade resistance, ensuring durability. In contrast, water-based markers use a dye or pigment suspended in water, making them non-toxic and easy to clean, suitable for children and everyday use. Each type caters to specific applications and user preferences.</p></p>
