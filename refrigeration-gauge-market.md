<p><p>The refrigeration gauge market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital gauges. Analog gauges feature a mechanical dial and needle that indicate pressure levels, offering a straightforward, easy-to-read display. They are typically favored for their simplicity and reliability. In contrast, digital gauges provide precise readings on an electronic screen, often including advanced features like data logging and connectivity options. Digital models are increasingly popular due to their accuracy and ease of use, catering to modern refrigeration needs.</p></p>
