<p><ul><li>Oil Immersed Transformer</li><li>Dry-type Transformer</li><li>Box-type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV transformer market encompasses three primary types: oil-immersed transformers, dry-type transformers, and box-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers are common in large-scale applications, offering better cooling and efficiency but requiring more maintenance. Dry-type transformers are suitable for indoor installations, providing safety and low environmental impact. Box-type transformers combine features of both, offering compactness and ease of installation while ensuring protection against harsh conditions. Each type serves specific solar power applications, catering to diverse operational and environmental needs.</p></p>
