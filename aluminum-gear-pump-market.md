<p><p>The aluminum gear pump market is categorized into three primary types: internal aluminum gear pumps, external aluminum gear pumps, and others. Internal aluminum gear pumps feature gears that operate within a closed housing, providing efficient fluid transfer with low pulsation and noise. External aluminum gear pumps have gears mounted outside the housing, typically used for higher flow rates and ease of maintenance. "Others" encompasses specialized designs or hybrid pumps tailored for specific industrial applications, enhancing versatility across various sectors.</p></p>
