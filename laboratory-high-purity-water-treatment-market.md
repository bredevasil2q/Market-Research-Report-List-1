<p><p>The laboratory high-purity water treatment market comprises three main types: Ultra-Pure Water, Analytical-Grade Water, and Laboratory-Grade Water. Ultra-Pure Water, often used in semiconductor manufacturing and pharmaceuticals, has exceptionally low contaminant levels. Analytical-Grade Water is suitable for precise laboratory analysis and testing, ensuring minimal interference with results. Laboratory-Grade Water is generally used for routine lab procedures and equipment cleaning, meeting specific purity standards but being less stringent than the other two grades. Each type serves distinct applications within scientific research and industry.</p></p>
