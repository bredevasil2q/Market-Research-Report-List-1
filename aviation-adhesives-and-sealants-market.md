<p><p>The aviation adhesives and sealants market comprises two main types: solvent-based and water-based formulations. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents to dissolve the adhesive components, providing strong bonding characteristics suitable for high-performance applications in harsh environments. In contrast, water-based adhesives focus on using water as the primary solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types are pivotal in ensuring the safety and durability of aircraft structures and components.</p></p>
