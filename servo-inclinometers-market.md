<p><p>The servo inclinometers market is divided into single-axis and dual-axis types. Single-axis servo inclinometers measure tilt in one direction, making them suitable for applications requiring simple orientation data. In contrast, dual-axis servo inclinometers can assess tilt in two perpendicular directions, providing more comprehensive measurements for complex positioning tasks. These devices are essential in various sectors, including construction, aerospace, and robotics, where accurate angle detection is critical for stability, positioning, and alignment.</p></p>
