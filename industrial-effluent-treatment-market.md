<p><strong>The Industrial Effluent Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Treatment</li><li>Off-site Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial effluent treatment market encompasses two primary types: on-site and off-site treatment. On-site treatment involves managing wastewater directly at the facility, utilizing technologies like biological treatment or membrane filtration to reduce pollutants before discharge. This method offers greater control over the treatment process and minimizes transportation costs. Off-site treatment, on the other hand, involves transporting effluent to specialized facilities for processing, leveraging advanced technologies and economies of scale. Each approach caters to varying regulatory compliance and environmental sustainability needs.</p></p>
