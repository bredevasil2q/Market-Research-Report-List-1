<p><strong>The Low Pressure Galvanized Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe</li><li>Cold Galvanized Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Pressure Galvanized Pipe market is primarily categorized into two types: Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe and Cold Galvanized Pipe. Hot Dip Galvanized Pipe involves immersing steel in molten zinc, providing a robust protective coating that withstands harsh environments, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Cold Galvanized Pipe uses a zinc electroplating process to achieve a thinner layer of protection, suitable for less demanding conditions. Both types offer corrosion resistance, but their application depends on specific environmental and structural requirements.</p></p>
