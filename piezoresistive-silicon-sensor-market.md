<p><strong>The Piezoresistive Silicon Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoresistive silicon sensor market includes relative (gauge) pressure sensors and differential pressure sensors. Relative pressure sensors measure pressure concerning atmospheric pressure, providing real-time data for applications like HVAC and automotive systems. In contrast, differential pressure sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, crucial for flow measurement and filtration systems. Both types utilize the piezoresistive effect in silicon materials to transform mechanical stress into electrical signals, enabling precise pressure monitoring in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
