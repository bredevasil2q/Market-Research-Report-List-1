<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) insulation is divided into two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell spray polyurethane foam is a lightweight and flexible option that provides excellent sound absorption and thermal insulation, making it suitable for interior applications. In contrast, closed cell spray polyurethane foam offers superior thermal resistance, moisture barrier properties, and structural support due to its dense composition, making it ideal for exterior applications and areas requiring high insulation performance. Both types enhance energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
