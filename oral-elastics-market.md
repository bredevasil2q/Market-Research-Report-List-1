<p><ul><li>Latex</li><li>Non-latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral elastics market is segmented into two main types: latex and non-latex. Latex oral elastics are made from natural rubber, offering strength, flexibility, and resilience in orthodontic treatments. They are commonly used due to their effectiveness and cost-efficiency. Non-latex oral elastics, typically made from materials like polyurethane or silicone, cater to patients with latex allergies, providing a hypoallergenic alternative. This diversification accommodates various patient needs, enhancing comfort while maintaining the efficacy of orthodontic procedures.</p></p>
