<p><p>Online accounting systems can be categorized into three main market types: </p><p>1. **Browser-Based**: Accessible through web browsers, these systems require no installation and offer flexibility, allowing users to access their accounts from any device with internet connectivity.</p><p>2. **SaaS (Software as a Service)**: This model includes online accounting software hosted in the cloud, providing subscription-based access, automatic updates, and maintenance, making it cost-effective for businesses.</p><p>3. **Application Service Providers (ASPs)**: ASPs deliver accounting applications over the internet, managing hardware and software, which allows companies to focus on their core activities while benefiting from remote access to accounting tools.</p></p>
