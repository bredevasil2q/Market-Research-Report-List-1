<p><p>The polycarbonate glazing market is categorized into general grade and high transparency types. General grade polycarbonate offers basic impact resistance and durability, making it suitable for a variety of standard applications like roofing and security. In contrast, high transparency polycarbonate is designed for applications requiring superior optical clarity and light transmission, such as retail displays and architectural features. Both types are valued for their lightweight nature and energy efficiency, appealing to diverse industries from construction to automotive.</p></p>
