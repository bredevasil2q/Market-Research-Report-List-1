<p><p>The E-CR glass fibers market is categorized into polymer composites and non-polymer composites. Polymer composites utilize E-CR glass fibers in resin systems, enhancing mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, commonly found in automotive, aerospace, and construction applications. Non-polymer composites, on the other hand, include the use of E-CR glass fibers in inorganic matrices, typically for high-temperature applications, serving industries like electrical insulation and infrastructure. Both types leverage the unique qualities of E-CR glass fibers to improve performance and durability.</p></p>
