<p><strong>The Electronic Descaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Non-capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic descaler market is divided into two main types: capacitive and non-capacitive. Capacitive electronic descalers use capacitive coupling technology to alter the ionic charge of mineral particles in water, preventing scale formation on pipes and appliances. Non-capacitive descalers, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic fields or other physical mechanisms to disrupt scale buildup. Both types aim to enhance the efficiency of water systems and prolong the lifespan of plumbing systems by reducing limescale accumulation.</p></p>
