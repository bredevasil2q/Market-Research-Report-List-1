<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capsule coffee machines are categorized into two main market types: closed source systems and open source systems. Closed source systems use proprietary capsules that are designed specifically for a particular machine brand, ensuring consistent quality but limiting options. In contrast, open source systems accommodate a variety of compatible capsules from different brands, offering greater flexibility and choice for consumers. This distinction affects user experience, convenience, and cost, influencing purchasing decisions in the rapidly evolving coffee market.</p></p>
