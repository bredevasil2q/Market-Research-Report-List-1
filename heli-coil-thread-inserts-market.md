<p><p>Heli-Coil thread inserts are used to repair or enhance internal threads in metals, providing a durable, strong connection. The market is divided into two main types: tanged and tangless thread inserts. Tanged thread inserts feature a small tang for easy installation and removal, ensuring secure placement. In contrast, tangless thread inserts remove the tang to create a smooth, flush finish, ideal for applications where space is restricted or appearance matters. Both types cater to various industrial and automotive needs.</p></p>
