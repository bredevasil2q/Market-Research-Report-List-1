<p><p>The ammonia absorption chillers market is segmented into various types based on their critical components. The evaporator facilitates refrigerant evaporation, absorbing heat from the surroundings. The absorber blends ammonia with water, concentrating the refrigerant solution. The pump circulates the solution between components, ensuring efficient operation. The generator converts the solution back to refrigerant using heat. Analyzers monitor system performance and efficiency. Other components may include heat exchangers and control systems, enhancing the overall functionality of absorption chillers.</p></p>
