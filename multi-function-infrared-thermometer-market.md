<p><p>The multi-function infrared thermometer market includes two primary types: multi-function ear infrared thermometers and multi-function forehead infrared thermometers. Ear infrared thermometers use infrared sensors to measure the temperature inside the ear canal, providing quick and accurate readings, making them ideal for pediatric use. Forehead infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature from the skin surface of the forehead, offering non-invasive and instant measurements. Both types cater to the growing demand for convenient, rapid temperature monitoring in healthcare and home settings.</p></p>
