<p><p>Deicing and anti-icing equipment are crucial for maintaining safe operations in winter conditions. Mechanical de-icing systems utilize physical methods, such as brushes or pneumatic blowers, to remove ice accumulation from surfaces. In contrast, electric pulse de-icing systems employ rapid electric pulses to generate heat, effectively melting ice without damaging the surface. Both types enhance safety in aviation, roadways, and other sectors, ensuring optimal performance and minimizing disruptions caused by winter weather conditions.</p></p>
