<p><p>The choledochoscope market is segmented into rigid and flexible types. Rigid choledochoscopes are designed for direct visualization of the bile duct, offering precise imaging during surgical procedures. They are often used in surgeries that require a stable instrument for clear access. In contrast, flexible choledochoscopes provide greater maneuverability and adaptability in navigating complex anatomical structures, making them ideal for endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) and other minimally invasive interventions, enhancing patient outcomes in biliary tract diseases.</p></p>
