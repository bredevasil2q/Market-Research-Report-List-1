<p><ul><li>Powder-Free Disposable Nitrile Gloves</li><li>Powdered Disposable Nitrile Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable nitrile gloves market is segmented into two main types: powder-free and powdered disposable nitrile gloves. Powder-free disposable nitrile gloves are designed for those who prefer a non-irritating option, ideal for medical and food handling applications. They offer excellent tactile sensitivity and reduce the risk of contamination. Conversely, powdered disposable nitrile gloves are coated with a light dusting of powder, aiding in easier donning and doffing, making them suitable for industrial and heavy-duty use, despite potential allergy concerns.</p></p>
