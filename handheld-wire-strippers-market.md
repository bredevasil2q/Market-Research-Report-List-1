<p><p>Handheld wire strippers are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual wire strippers require users to manually adjust and operate the tool, offering simplicity and control but demanding more effort. Semi-automatic models streamline the stripping process with spring-loaded mechanisms, balancing efficiency and user input. Fully automatic strippers eliminate manual effort altogether, using motors and sensors to quickly and accurately strip wires. Each type caters to different user needs, from casual DIY enthusiasts to professional electricians.</p></p>
